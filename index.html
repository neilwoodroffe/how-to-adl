Withdraw money from an ATM   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Place your bankcard and money into your wallet
  • Take out your bankcard from your wallet/purse
  • Go to an ATM
  • Insert your bankcard in the ATM
  • Enter the amount of money you wish to withdraw
  • Enter your PIN
  • Choose the transaction and account type
  • When dispensed, take out your bankcard, money and account balance

Grocery shopping   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Go to the checkout to pay for the groceries
  • Go to the grocery store
  • Pack your groceries away in the pantry and fridge
  • Write a shopping list
  • Obtain a shopping trolley or basket
  • Navigate through the store to find the required groceries using your shopping list
  • Take the groceries home
  • Check your pantry and fridge for groceries that you may need
  • Check your shopping list to ensure you have found all of the groceries you need

Cooking chicken stirfry   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Add vegetables, sauce and stir regularly
  • Serve stirfry with noodles or rice
  • Cook chicken until light golden brown
  • Cut up the chicken and vegetables
  • Turn heat down to low-medium heat and simmer until vegetables are cooked
  • Turn off the hotplate
  • Add oil to pan and turn on the hotplate to a medium-high heat
  • Get your ingredients ready
  • Wash your hands

Laundry   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Add laundry powder and close the washing machine door
  • Once dry fold the clothes and put them away
  • Press the start button
  • Empty the clean wet clothes into a basket
  • Set the washing machine to wash cycle
  • Load the washing machine with the dirty clothing
  • Once finished open the washing machine door
  • Hang the wet clothes onto the clothes line
  • Collect your dirty clothing

Cleaning the floor   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Pick up the dustpan and brush
  • Using the mop and bucket mop the floor
  • Empty the dustpan into the bin
  • Pick up the broom
  • Sweep the piles of dust into the dustpan
  • Empty the mob bucket and wait for the floor to dry
  • Fill the bucket with hot water and add floor cleaner
  • Sweep the dust on the floor into piles

Catch a bus to an unfamiliar destination   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Hail the bus with the chosen bus route number
  • Make sure you have enough funds on your go card
  • Push the bell when you are at the destination
  • Search the Translink website for available bus routes and times
  • Go to the nearest bus stop on the chosen bus route
  • Swipe your go card as you leave the bus
  • Swipe your go card as you hop on the bus and take a seat

Go to the doctor to get a prescription   

Click, drag and drop to place these steps in the correct order

  • Present to the doctor's surgery for your scheduled appointment
  • Attend your local pharmacy to have your prescription filled
  • Tell the medical admin that you have arrived
  • Present back to the medical admin to pay your bill or sign the medicare form
  • Wait in the waiting room until the doctor calls your name
  • Phone the doctor's surgery to book an appointment
  • Take your medication home and store it in a safe place
  • Tell your doctor that you require a prescription for your medication
